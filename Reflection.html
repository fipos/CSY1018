Reflection

looking back at when I started this programme at the tail end of last year (2017). Everything that was being taught in class was really not sticking but thanks to the passion I have got burning in me and the support I have received from my lecturers has enhanced the drive to spend more time on my course assignments and other related academic work. This module has not only added to me as a person but has also changed my sense of reasoning. Since I started this module, I have realised that my dream as a business computing analyst can be achieved if I am able to give a significant amount of commitment to my school work. The web development module has given me the opportunity to be able to design, develop and maintain a web site. It has also given me a broader knowledge and understanding of what a quality and insightful web site looks like. I have also imbibed in myself the knowledge that, a lot of successful organisation has benefited enormously from the output of their organisational web page. This has also enhanced the relationship and communication between these organisations and their prospective customers. This has also supported the level of organisational productive and profitability which believe I will be part of someday. I will really want to develop some additional knowledge and skills in this area of my programme.
